Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Wind-Up Music Box

On the Subject of Wind-Up Music Box

Bringing mechanical to practical to digital

/!\ IMPORTANT /!\
This is a module in the Wind-Up series. Refer to Appendix WIND-UP KEY for important notes.

  • The module holds a single key hole in the top left and 4 music buttons on the bottom.
  • To solve, use the given notes and 2 sequence keys to unscramble and submit 12 notes.
  • Notes can only be entered while idle. Wind to clear your sequence.

  • When wound, the music box will play 2 six-note sequences, heard as 12 notes
  • Both the music and buttons use only 4 notes and share labels between the buttons and guide
  • Notes are marked low to high in reading order

OBTAINING SEQUENCE KEYS

Conditions
NOTE SEQ A SEQ B
1 Digit 1 of SN Total ♩ Notes
2 Digit 2 of SN Total Vowels in INDs
3 Digit 3 of SN Total ♪ Notes
4 Digit 4 of SN Total # of Ports
5 Digit 5 of SN Total ♬ Notes
6 Digit 6 of SN Total ♫ Notes
  • Always convert letters to numbers (A = 1)
  • Always MOD 6 your calculations
  • If a key is the same as any previous key for that sequence, +1 until unique, staying within 0-5
  • The first key is assigned to the first note, the second to the second, etc.

DECRYPTING

  • Swap notes from Sequence A with Sequence B with matching keys
  • Enter the resulting twelve-note sequence

Appendix WIND-UP KEY

Keep your friends close, and your keys closer...

This section goes over the mechanics of Wind-Up Key modules

  • Only one key will appear in the mission
  • It will always spawn on the first bomb
  • The key must be used across multiple modules, across multiple bombs, when applicable
  • If the key is already placed in a hole, you must pick it up before moving it to another hole

DO NOT LOSE YOUR KEY
YOU HAVE BEEN WARNED

Note: due to the nature of these modules, controllers are not recommended, but the support is there
